How it works

  • Income and recurring payments happen on the 1st day of each month.
  • The model holds no separate cash. Monthly surpluses add to investments and monthly deficits withdraw from investments.
  • Investment growth is applied on the last day of the month to the opening invested balance only.
  • Yearly results are shown by default. Expand any year to inspect monthly details.
